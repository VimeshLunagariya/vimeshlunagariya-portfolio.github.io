Why Me ?


A cheap smartphone with a cheap internet plan is now in the hands of every Indian adult. For many people, their first experience of the Internet is through a smartphone screen. Thus, having an app has now become as important as a website for your business.

Being an experienced mobile app developer, I am always on a mission to balance the client's expectations as well as the end user for the app for the client's business. As I have 3 years of experience with mobile app development. I am a full-time Freelancer who mainly works on mobile app development. I have always integrated a systematic process in app development -from initial requirements gathering, to designing the architecture/framework for the app, to coding the app, to designing. That, the implementation, testing and finally fixing of any error verbatim. Recently, I have developed cross-platform applications that are also coded in such a way that they work on both Android and iOS platforms.

I have already built next-gen, user-centric, supremely intuitive mobile & web apps based on the layered architecture of Flutter. Exploit Google's SDK & Flutter 3 to envision ROI booster apps. I have 13+ Flutter apps and I competently use its single codebase, SQLite, GetIt, Provider, Bloc, Dart, SOLID, & Restful APIs to design impeccable cross-platform apps & networks in a set timeframe.

I'm self-guided and responsible who comprehend the significance of top-notched applications. React Native is great for mobile apps. It provides a slick, smooth, and responsive user interface, while significantly reducing load time. It's also much faster and cheaper to build apps in React Native as opposed to building native ones, without the need to compromise on quality and functionality. React Native also allows you to easily incorporate third-party plug-ins and APIs, including maps and payment systems.

Cross-platform has transformed the development of mobile apps making them available on all screens like Android, iOS, and web with beautiful UI & speed. Coupling the multi-platform capabilities & native features, I'm mastered to serve you with exceptional apps.
